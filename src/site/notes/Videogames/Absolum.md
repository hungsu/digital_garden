![[absolum banner.webm]]

Sidescrolling beat em up like Castle Crashers and Streets of Rage 2, but also a [[Unsorted/roguelite\|roguelite]] with [[Videogames/Hades by Supergiant\|Hades]]-style [[Unsorted/engine builder\|power-up drafting]].

Developed by [Guard Crush Games](https://streetsoffury.wixsite.com/guardcrushgames) and [[Dotemu\|Dotemu]]

Has absolutely gorgeous art, by [Supamonks](https://www.supamonks.com/video-games)

[Soundtrack](https://garethcoker.bandcamp.com/album/absolum-original-game-soundtrack) is fantastic, by [Gareth Coker](https://www.gareth-coker.net/), who also did music for Ori and the Will of the Wisps, Halo Infinite, and ARK.

Has an active [subreddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/ABSOLUM/)
